\TermineGlossario{E-commerce}
\DefinizioneGlossario{Is the activity of electronically buying or selling products on online services or over the Internet. It also includes the money transfers and the data necessary to execute these transactions.}
\\
\TermineGlossario{ESlint}
\DefinizioneGlossario{ESLint is a static code analysis tool for identifying problematic patterns found in JavaScript code.}
