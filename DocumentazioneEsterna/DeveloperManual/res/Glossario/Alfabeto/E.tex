\subsection{E}
\TermineGlossario{E-commerce}
\DefinizioneGlossario{It is the activity of electronically buying or selling  products on online services or over the Internet. It also includes the money transfers and the data necessary to execute these transactions.}
\\
\TermineGlossario{ESlint}
\DefinizioneGlossario{ESLint is a static code analysis tool for identifying problematic patterns found in JavaScript code. It allows to maintain a good level of coding quality easily.}
\\
