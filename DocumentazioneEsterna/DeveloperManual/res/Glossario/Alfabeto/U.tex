\subsection{U}
\TermineGlossario{UML}
\DefinizioneGlossario{The Unified Modeling Language is a general-purpose, developmental, modeling language in the field of software engineering that is intended to provide a standard way to visualize the design of a system. UML 2.0 is the second version of UML, made official in 2005 after a revision and expansion of the meta-model and the introduction of new types of diagrams.}
